Intercultural Communications Report

TECM 4180 is an advanced TECM course that teaches students about how to gather data through surveys and interviews and to present your findings concisely. The intercultural communications report was one of the major assignments for this class that sought to display how a global franchise like McDonald’s website translates to other countries. The report would go into detail about the culture of the country we were assigned and how the marketing strategy respects of disrespects the culture.

Skills Demonstrated

  • Technical Editing
  • Research Ability
  • Report Writing

Programs Used

  • Microsoft Word
  • Hofstede Insights

For my country, I was given Vietnam. Using Hofstede Insight’s six cultural dimensions of:

  • Power Distance
  • Individualism/Collectivism
  • Masculine/Feminine
  • Uncertainty Avoidance
  • Long-term Oreination
  • Indulgence

I was able to determine whether McDonald’s marketing strategies aligned with those of Vietnam. Additionally, research was done to help better understand Vietnam’s rich culture and how their culture has evolved over time.
